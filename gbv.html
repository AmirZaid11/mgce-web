Understanding GBV

Gender-based violence (GBV) is a violation of human rights and a global public health crisis. It includes physical, emotional, sexual, and economic abuse. In South Africa and across the continent, women and girls face disproportionate risks. This platform stands for awareness, solidarity, and action.

Myth vs Fact

Myth

GBV only affects women.

Fact

GBV affects all genders, but women are disproportionately impacted.

Myth

GBV is a private issue, not a public concern.

Fact

GBV is a human rights violation and a public health crisis.

Myth

Only physical violence counts as GBV.

Fact

GBV includes emotional, sexual, and economic abuse too.
